Key Insights
C Programming: With the highest priority, C Programming remains fundamental to all major coding interviews and development roles.
OOPs with C++: Object-Oriented Programming concepts are essential, and mastering C++ can greatly enhance problem-solving abilities.
Writing - I: Communication and writing skills are critical in conveying technical ideas clearly and concisely.
Linear Algebra and Differential Calculus: These mathematical concepts form the backbone of machine learning and data science. They are essential for understanding algorithms, data transformations, and optimization processes used in AI and predictive modeling.
Differential Equations and Integral Calculus: Crucial for solving complex problems in data science, physics, and engineering. These concepts are heavily utilized in modeling dynamic systems, neural networks, and real-world simulations.
